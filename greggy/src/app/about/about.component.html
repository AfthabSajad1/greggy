about

greggy is a design forward ceramic smoker ware brand crafting objects for your modern rituals, objects that elevate the smoking experience through form, intention and craft. the core language of greggy is derived from functionalism, simplicity and integration of art and craft.

it exists as a testament of it's founder's, Amit Eapen's, love for midcentury ceramics and the vision for a sustainable design oriented smoking lifestyle. Each piece is handmade by skilled pottery artisans in rural India, combining Eapen’s design sensibility with generations of craftsmanship. The result is smoker ware that feels sculptural, tactile, and deeply personal — meant to be used, displayed, and loved.
